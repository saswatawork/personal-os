## Scoring Rubric

Each topic is scored 0-100 across three dimensions:
- **Explain (40pts):** Can you explain the concept clearly?
- **Build (30pts):** Did you complete the associated build task?
- **Apply (30pts):** Can you apply it to a novel problem?

**Completion threshold:** 80/100. Below 80 means the topic needs more work before moving on.
